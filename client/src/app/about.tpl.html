About the Project

This project is aimed at developing an online application for the Training and Placement Department of the college. The "Bidding Management System" has been developed to keep an accurate record and management of the Bids performed by the user bidders who bid on various web platforms like Freelancer, Upwork, Fiverr etc on behalf of software services companies. This project will help organizations to track the bids made by different bidders in their company, have access to data like bid amount, project technologies, project type etc and hence provide different types of analytics into both project and bids data. Therefore, it functions fully and independently. This will enable the tender to learn more detailed information about the bidder under the framework .It is openness, justice and fairness. The function of the system is to save, consult, modify and delete the information of the bidder and provide a platform where admin need not to visit each website to see the performance of bidders about latest work done, so the function is mainly concentrated on the reading and operating the information. This project will provide both the admins and the employers a best platform to do their work As the technology is being advanced the way of life is changing accordangly. Now people will find work according to their performance and capabilities. .

Importance of Bid Management System

Technologies trends in the market (Which can enable company to find out which technologies to most bid on).

Business according to each technology. (In finding out, working on which technology brings the most business to the company and hence can help defining strategy of the company for future).

The proposed system can also be put on online web server for its usage by companies.

This system leverage a single comprehensive system to provide all of the functionalities like project management, Bid submitting and submission review, Analytics.
